Some common questions
were often asked

My energy drops in the afternoon—what can I do?

Try incorporating balanced meals with slow-releasing carbs, lean proteins, and healthy fats. A nutritious lunch can help you stay focused and avoid that midday crash.

Why am I always hungry between meals?

You might not be getting enough fiber or protein. These help keep you full longer. Snacking on fruits, nuts, or yogurt can also help curb cravings.

How do I start eating healthier on a budget?

Plan your meals ahead, buy in bulk, and focus on whole foods like rice, beans, veggies, and eggs. Simple swaps can make a big difference without breaking the bank.

What foods help with studying and concentration?

Foods rich in omega-3s (like walnuts), antioxidants (like berries), and complex carbs (like oats) are great for brain function and focus.

I don’t have time to cook—what are my options?

Look for quick recipes with 5 ingredients or less, or prep meals in batches on the weekend. Healthy eating doesn’t have to be time-consuming.

How do I stay consistent with healthy eating?

Set realistic goals, track your progress, and don’t stress about perfection. Consistency comes from building habits—not restriction.
