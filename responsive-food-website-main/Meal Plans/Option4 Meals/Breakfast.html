Fruit & Chia Pudding

Why You Should Eat This

Fruit & chia pudding is a refreshing and nutrient-packed snack or breakfast. The chia seeds provide fiber and healthy fats, while the fresh fruit adds natural sweetness and antioxidants, making it a satisfying and energizing choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • ½ cup milk (any type)
  • ½ cup diced fruit

Instructions

  1. Soak chia seeds in milk overnight.
  2. Top with fruit in the morning.
