Greek Yogurt with Berries & Nuts

Why You Should Eat This

Greek yogurt with berries and nuts is a nutritious and satisfying option for breakfast or a snack. It’s packed with protein, healthy fats, and antioxidants—giving you lasting energy and supporting overall health, all in one tasty bowl.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup plain Greek yogurt
  • ½ cup mixed berries
  • 1 tbsp chopped almonds

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Chill or serve immediately.

Nutrition Facts

Serving Size 1 Serving
Calories 350
Total Fat 32g
Saturated Fat 3g
Cholesterol 10mg
Sodium 70mg
