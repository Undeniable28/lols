Grilled Chicken and Beans

Grilled chicken and beans make a protein-packed, satisfying lunch. The lean chicken provides essential protein, while the beans offer fiber and nutrients, making it a balanced and energizing meal to keep you fueled through the afternoon.

Ingredients

  • 100g grilled chicken breast
  • ½ cup black beans
  • ½ cup corn
  • Spices for flavor

Instructions

  1. Grill chicken.
  2. Mix with beans and corn; warm together.

Nutrition Facts

Serving Size 1 Serving
Calories 430
Total Fat 12g
Saturated Fat 2g
Cholesterol 60mg
Sodium 270mg
