Egg Muffins with Spinach & Cheese

Why You Should Eat This

Egg muffins with spinach and cheese make a delicious and nutritious breakfast. They’re packed with protein, fiber, and essential vitamins, while the cheese adds a savory richness. Perfect for a quick and filling start to your day!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup chopped spinach
  • 1 tbsp grated cheese

Instructions

  1. Mix ingredients and pour into muffin tins.
  2. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 15–20 mins.
