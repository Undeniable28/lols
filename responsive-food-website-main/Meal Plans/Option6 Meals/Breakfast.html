Banana Peanut Butter Toast

Why You Should Eat This

Banana peanut butter toast is a simple yet energizing breakfast. The creamy peanut butter provides healthy fats and protein, while the banana adds natural sweetness and potassium, giving you a tasty and balanced start to your day.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice whole wheat bread
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • Â½ banana (sliced)

Instructions

  1. Toast bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter and top with banana.
