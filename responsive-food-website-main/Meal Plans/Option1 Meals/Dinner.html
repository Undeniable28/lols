Pork Adobo

Why You Should Eat This

Pork Adobo is a delicious and hearty dish that is rich in flavor and provides essential nutrients. It's a great source of protein and can be enjoyed with rice for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • Garlic
  • Dried Bay Leaves
  • Vinegar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Peppercorn
  • Water
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Combine the pork belly, soy sauce, and garlic then marinade for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat the pot and put in the marinated pork belly. Cook this all up for a few minutes.
  3. Pour the remaining marinade including the garlic.
  4. Add water, whole peppercorn, and dried bay leaves. Bring to a boil and simmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour.
  5. Add vinegar and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes.
  6. Add salt to taste.
  7. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!
