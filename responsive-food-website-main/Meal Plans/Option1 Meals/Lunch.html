Egg Fried Rice

Why You Should Eat This

Egg fried rice is a flavorful and comforting lunch option that's both filling and easy to enjoy. Packed with protein from the eggs and energy-boosting carbs from the rice, it's a well-rounded meal that keeps you energized and satisfied through the afternoon. Add some veggies, and you've got a balanced, delicious lunch in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tsp soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the cooked rice and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes to heat through.
  4. Push the rice to one side of the pan. Crack the egg into the empty side and scramble until fully cooked.
  5. Mix the scrambled egg into the rice evenly.
  6. Add soy sauce and stir well. Cook for another minute.
  7. Serve hot.
