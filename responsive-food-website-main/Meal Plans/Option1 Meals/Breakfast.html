Peanut Butter Banana Sandwich

Why You Should Eat This

A peanut butter banana sandwich is a quick and wholesome breakfast that gives you the energy to start your day strong. The natural sweetness of bananas pairs perfectly with the rich, creamy peanut butter, offering a satisfying mix of healthy fats, protein, and fiber to keep you full and focused all morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole wheat bread
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 banana (sliced)

Instructions

  1. Toast both slices of whole wheat bread if you prefer a crunchier texture.
  2. Spread 1 tablespoon of peanut butter on each slice.
  3. Slice the banana into thin, even rounds.
  4. Layer the banana slices on one slice of the bread.
  5. Top with the second slice, peanut butter side down.
  6. Slice in half and serve immediately
